You can also choose for a field to be faceted or not by checking the fields facet tick box. A faceted field will be counted in the result set e.g. if 'colour' was faceted, then the search results could indicate that there are 10 red items, 4 blue items and 1 green item.

When you have made your decision, click the 'Import' button to begin the import process.
